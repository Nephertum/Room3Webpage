Positive contributions Natural Language Processing makes to society

  • Natural language processing (NLP) is a field of artificial intelligence where computational methods are used analyze and interpret human language. NLP encompasses all algorithms that process natural language, this ranges from algorithms designed to translate spoken commands into instructions for the program to execute to an speech synthesis algorithm.
  • The most wide spread application of NLP is virtual assistants
